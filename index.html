TCX3212 Predictive Analytics

Semester 2 2025/26 | National University of Singapore
Instructor: Samantha Sow, Department of Information Systems and Analytics

Course Description​

This course covers predictive analytics techniques using Python, from foundational programming to advanced time series forecasting. Topics span data preparation, regression models, classification, and forecasting.
