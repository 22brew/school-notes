## Course Overview

**TCX3212 – Predictive Analytics** is an elective under the Business Analytics Focus Area of the Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) programme at NUS. It provides a solid grounding in predictive analytics — the use of statistical learning and machine learning techniques to build models that forecast outcomes from data.

**Instructor:** Samantha Sow, Senior Lecturer, Department of Information Systems and Analytics, NUS.

---

## What is Predictive Analytics?

Predictive analytics sits at the intersection of **statistical learning** and **machine learning**:

- **Statistical learning** emphasises model interpretability, precision, and uncertainty quantification.
- **Machine learning** emphasises large-scale data, application performance, and prediction accuracy.

The distinction between the two has become increasingly blurred. This course focuses on the statistical learning perspective while drawing on Python as the primary implementation tool.
